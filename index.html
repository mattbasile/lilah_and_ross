    early October IS ONE OF THE MOST BEAUTIFUL TIMES TO VISIT MAINE, BUT IT IS CHILLY! EXPECT highS of 60-65F AND LOWS OF 45F. THE RECEPTION WILL BE HEATED! THE CEREMONY WILL NOT... However, we have it on good authority that no benevolent god WILL let iT rain on Oct 6th or 7th 2023.
